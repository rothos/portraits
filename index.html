Portraits

As an ongoing art project, I sometimes ask people to draw me. This project has undergone long periods of stagnation, but I haven't ever given up on it. Currently, I have a total of 150 portraits collected on this page.

I welcome any portraits you want to send me, and I will almost definitely include them on this page. (I have omitted a total of two portraits from this page (2/152, or 1.3%), for different reasons. Only in very special cases do I not include a portrait.)

For details about how I processed the images and created this gallery, see the very bottom of the page.
